Information on the weekly crossword mailout

Update of Puzzles on the Site

When I first started this website, I was posting a new 'regular' and 'something different' puzzle each week. Recently, as I have found myself short of time and skipping a week from time to time, I considered only posting new puzzles every two weeks. Instead I have decided to post one new puzzle every week, alternately updating the 'regular' and 'something different' puzzles.

Weekly email

When I was posting two new puzzles each week, I was only emailing my subscribers the 'regular' of the two. However, in order to maintain a weekly supply under my new arrangements, I will now be sending out each new puzzle. If you really don't want to receive one or the other of the varieties, please let me know and I can arrange a split mailing list. Or you can just ignore the puzzles you don't like! I should mention that the less conventional puzzles are more likely to contain more complex html formatting and sometimes even images, making them larger and less universally portable.
